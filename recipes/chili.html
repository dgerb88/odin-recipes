Chili

Description:

I mean it's chili. What more do you need to know. Bunch of beans, meat, and deliciousness in a pot. Let marinate and then serve with chips.

Ingredients

  • beans
  • meat
  • garlic
  • onion
  • tomato sauce
  • 1 can love

Directions:

  1. cook meat
  2. chop onion and garlic
  3. Add everything together and let cook for 4 hours
